Background Information

OBESITY

Obesity has become an issue nowadays and it has been noted that it exceeded 42.4 percent in 2020. Obesity affects the body physically, increasing blood pressure and many other conditions like extra cholesterol, high blood pressure. It is very important to follow healthy diet habits in order to reduce obesity. There are many methods to improve both underweight and overweight BMIs. It has been proven that weight management has been very useful when people eat and exercise according to their Body Mass Index requirements.
